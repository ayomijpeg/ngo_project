Know About Us

We help underprivileged sections of society to uplift

We work for the development of the Bastis in the Varanasi region of UP, helping in education, health, and finance. We continuously organize events and awareness sessions in those areas, making them aware of government schemes, scholarships, and health. We aim to uplift those underprivileged sections of society.

What We Do

Conducts Workshops

Initiating skill development programs (for men and women) and organizing leadership/personality development programs, making them aware of government schemes, scholarships, and health.

Skills Training

Facilitating power through the generation of development opportunities, income-generating activities, providing skill-based training, and imparting new technology in agriculture, etc.

Activities and Awareness Camps

Development of women and rural folks socially, economically, and politically through income-generating activities, education programs, and awareness camps.

Runs Educational Programmes

Running educational programs like formal education, non-formal education, and adult education programs to decrease illiteracy and improve human resources for the development of a nation.
